## MacGyver Sessions: Novel, Exciting, Self-Made, Hacked, and/or Improvised Sensors, Data Acquisition, and Data Transmission Solutions for Earth and Space Science
The MacGyver Sessions are sessions at the American Geophysical Union Fall Meeting and European Geophysical Union meetings which are dedicated to the use of free and open-source hardware and software in Earth and space sciences.
